Cyber Attacks/Threats

Click on the images below to learn about different types of cyber attacks!



Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) Attack

Restricts user access to resources by flooding the traffic, controlled by a botnet. The attacker instructs the botnet to overwhelm a server, causing it to reach full capacity and preventing users from accessing the website.



Man in the Middle (MitM) Attack

Happens when an attacker intercepts and possibly alters communication between two parties without their knowledge, allowing themselves to eavesdrop, capture sensitive data, or impersonate one party to manipulate the information.



Password Attack

Used to find or crack a password using Dictionary Attacks, Brute Force Attacks, Keylogger Attacks, Shoulder Surfing, and Rainbow Tables.



Email Attack

Malicious attempts to compromise email accounts, often through phishing, spoofing, or email attachments. Their main goals are to steal information or gain unauthorized access.
